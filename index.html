A Weekend of Remembrance

Join us for the 2026 Memorial Weekend Tribute at Woodward Park in Manteca, California. This three-day event honors the brave men and women who made the ultimate sacrifice in service to our nation.

Through ceremony, community, and reflection, we come together to ensure their legacy lives on. This isn't just an event—it's a promise that they will never be forgotten.

Our Mission: Honor, Remember, and Serve

Mission Statement

The Not Forgotten Foundation is dedicated to honoring our fallen heroes, supporting Gold Star families, and ensuring that the sacrifices of our service members are remembered for generations to come.

"To consecrate and sanctify our comradeship by our devotion to mutual helpfulness."
— American Legion Post 249 Motto

Our History

Founded by veterans who understand the cost of freedom, our organization has been serving the community for over 15 years. What began as a small local gathering has grown into one of California's most respected Memorial Day tributes.

Each year, we bring together thousands of community members, veterans, active duty military, and Gold Star families to honor those who made the ultimate sacrifice. Through ceremonies, education, and community support, we ensure that their legacy lives on.

Our Core Values

Honor

We honor the service and sacrifice of all who have served

Service

We serve our veterans, their families, and our community

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do to honor their memory

We build community through shared remembrance and respect
