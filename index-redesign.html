About the Event

Join us for a three-day Memorial Weekend tribute honoring the brave men and women who made the ultimate sacrifice in service to our nation.

Through ceremony, community gatherings, and meaningful activities, we come together to ensure their legacy lives on. This weekend is a promise that they will never be forgotten.

Words From a Warrior

Though we have retired, our devotion lives on to Honor and Remember our Fallen, our active duty Soldiers and our Veterans.

Please continue to join us by standing up, speaking up and praying for them and their families and asking God to heal our land.

You will soon find out, giving back to others what you need God to give to you, sets you up to receive more than you ever thought possible.

Our History

For over 15 years, the American Legion Post 249 Foundation has hosted The Memorial Weekend, bringing together thousands of community members, veterans, and families to honor those who made the ultimate sacrifice.

What began as a small gathering has grown into one of California's most respected Memorial Day tributes, touching lives and keeping the memory of our heroes alive for future generations.
